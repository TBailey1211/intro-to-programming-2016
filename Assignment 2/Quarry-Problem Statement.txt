Assumptions:
One run of trucks at a time
2 types of trucks only
small Truck Ton = 5 ton
large Truck Ton = 10 ton


Input: 
num S Trucks
num L Trucks
price

Calculations:
small Truck Ton = num S Trucks * small Truck Ton
large Truck Ton = num L Trucks * large Truck Ton
total Ton = small Truck Ton + large Truck Ton
bill = total Ton * price

Output:
small Truck Ton
large Truck Ton
total Ton
bill
